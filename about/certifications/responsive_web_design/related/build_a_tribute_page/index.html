Barack Hussein Obama

Barack Hussein Obama II was the 44th president of the United States and served two full terms from 2009 to 2017. Obama is affiliated with the Democratic party. His vice president was Joe Biden. Before serving as president, Obama was with the US Senate since 2005.
Obama was born in 1961 in Honolulu, HI. Obama is married to Michelle Obama, and is a proud father of two children.
Obama has won many awards and prizes for his outstanding achievements, including the Nobel Peace prize in 2009.
